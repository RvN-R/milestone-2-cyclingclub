About Us

Rams Cycling Club is a friendly and diverse South West London cycling club. The club takes its name from the Rams Brewery, well known and industrial hub in Wandsworth, and the ancestral home of Youngs Brewery. Although we are a relativley young club only forming at the beginning of 2021, we are gaining traction and our list of memebers is growing by the day. At present we offer two rides, one on Saturday and the other mid week at Lunch Time. As we grow we have plans to offer training rides, club and open events and cycling trips.

Riding with a cycling club can seem daunting at first, so we require all new prospective members to attend one of our introductory rides. These rides are announced on our social media and we try and hold them twice a month. These are short club rides in whihc we look at things such as what to carry with you, how to ride in group formations, and basic road safety.
