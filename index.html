About Us

Rams Cycling Club is a friendly and diverse South West London cycling club. The club was set up by two brothers, who had only recently discovered their love of cycling. The club takes it's name from the Rams Brewery, a well known industrial hub in Wandsworth, and the ancestral home of the famous Youngs Brewery.

Although we are a relatively young club, only forming at the beginning of 2021, we are gaining traction and our list of members is growing by the day. With the year we have all just experienced, it has never been more obvious that cycling is a fantastic way to not only keep on top of your physical health, but your mental health as well. For many of our members, the recent year has presented a list of challenges, the lack of human interaction topping the list! The group has provided our members the opportunity to meet and interact with people who have varied likes, occupations and beliefs. The most popular club activity is our Club Rides. At present, we offer two rides, one on Saturday and the other mid week at Lunch Time. Cycling has massive benefits and our goal is to share that with the greater community. As we grow we have plans to offer training rides, cycling trips and events for the Wandsworth community.

Riding with a cycling club can seem daunting at first, so to help intergrate new members we offer introductory rides. These rides are announced on our social media and we try and hold them twice a month. These are short club rides in which we look at things such as what to carry with you, how to ride in group formations and basic road safety.

Club Rides

What's better then getting out on the road with your mates for a cycle? Here at Ram Cycling Club the Club Rides are our favourite thing to do together. It allows us to get to know each other and most importantly get out there and stay active.

We have two routes the first "Saturday Ride" takes place every Saturday starting at 9 am. The second "Lunch Time Ride" takes place ever Wednesday at 1pm. We will be adding more routes in the future so be sure to check back for more updates.
